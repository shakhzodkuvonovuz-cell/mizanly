### Critical items requiring immediate attention:
1. **donate.tsx L96-132**: Payment flow records donation before Stripe confirms -- real money at risk
2. **duet-create.tsx L133-155**: Double-tap race condition can crash camera
3. **duet-create.tsx L601-605**: Next button navigates with empty videoUri, no guard

### Cross-cutting issues (all 5 screens):
- **RTL support missing** in 4/5 screens (donate, downloads, drafts, duet-create)
- **Light mode hardcoded** in all 5 screens' StyleSheets
- **SafeAreaView missing** in 3 screens (donate, downloads, drafts) -- using hardcoded spacers
- **No haptic feedback** in drafts.tsx; inconsistent haptic in others
- **No pagination** in donate and drafts
