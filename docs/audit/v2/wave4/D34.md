### Critical patterns across all 5 screens

1. **`colors.text.*` instead of `tc.text.*`** (all 5 screens, ~35 style properties total): The `createStyles(tc)` pattern is used correctly for backgrounds and borders, but text colors consistently bypass the theme parameter. Every screen will have broken text visibility in a light theme.

2. **No RTL support** (4 of 5 screens): Only `settings.tsx` imports and uses RTL utilities. The other 4 screens hardcode `flexDirection: 'row'`, use `left:` instead of `start:`, and don't flip chevron icons.

3. **Silent mutation failures** (3 screens): `series-detail`, `series-discover`, and `settings` all fire mutations with no `onError` handler. Settings is the worst offender -- 8 toggle mutations with no rollback on failure.

4. **No `showToast` feedback** (4 of 5 screens): Only `settings.tsx` imports `showToast`. The other 4 screens provide zero user-facing feedback for errors or mutation results.
