### Critical/High Priority Items
1. **#33 (C):** Gift shop credits coins before Stripe payment confirmation -- free coins exploit.
2. **#46 (H):** Go Live button never starts a non-scheduled stream -- primary CTA broken.
3. **#47 (H):** Date picker is a non-functional placeholder -- scheduling broken.
4. **#13, #23 (H):** Error states in followers/following render outside ScreenErrorBoundary.
5. **#34 (H):** Gift history has no pagination -- performance bomb for active users.

### Recurring Patterns
- **Light theme (A1):** All 5 screens hardcode dark theme colors in StyleSheet. Partially overridden in JSX but not consistently.
- **No haptic (B10):** 3 of 5 screens (followed-topics, followers, following) have no haptic feedback at all.
- **No double-tap guard (B13):** 4 of 5 screens lack protection against rapid duplicate taps on mutation-triggering buttons.
- **Dead imports:** 4 of 5 screens have unused imports (Icon in followers/following, shadow+animation in gift-shop, useEffect+Platform+EmptyState in go-live).
- **fontWeight vs fontFamily:** followers, following, and go-live use raw `fontWeight` instead of `fonts.*` tokens.
