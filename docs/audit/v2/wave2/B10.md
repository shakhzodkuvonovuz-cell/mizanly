| 1 | H | `notifications.service.ts:20-56` | **getNotifications does not filter banned/deleted actors.** The query includes actors with `select: { id, username, displayName, avatarUrl, isVerified }` but never checks `actor.isBanned = false` or `actor.isDeleted = false`. | 2 | H | `notifications.service.ts:171-356` | **create() does not check if actor is banned or deleted.** The method checks blocks, mutes, and per-type settings but never verifies `actor.isBanned === false` or `actor.isDeleted === false`. | 3 | H | `notifications.service.ts:442-479` | **Unread notifications accumulate forever.** `cleanupOldNotifications` only deletes `isRead: true` + older than 90 days. At scale (10M users, avg 50 unread each = 500M rows), this is a table-size bomb. | Unbounded table growth; degraded query performance over time. | S |
| 4 | M | `schema.prisma:1983-2021` | **Missing @@index on Notification.commentId, circleId, conversationId, followRequestId.** The model has indexes on `postId`, `reelId`, `threadId`, `videoId` but not on `commentId`, `circleId`, `conversationId`, or `followRequestId`. | 10 | M | `schema.prisma:703` | **Dual push token storage: User.expoPushToken vs Device.pushToken.** The User model has `expoPushToken String?` (line 703) and the Device model has `pushToken String @unique` (line 2403). | 12 | L | `notifications.service.ts:284-298` | **Batched notification update clobbers body text.** When batching (e.g., multiple likes on same post within 30 min), line 287 sets `body: \`${params.body \|\| ''} and ${count} others\``.
