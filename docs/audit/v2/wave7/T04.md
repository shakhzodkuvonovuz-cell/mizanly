## Priority Fixes

1. **threads `canReply()`** — 5 permission branches controlling who can reply. Authorization logic must be tested.
2. **threads `createContinuation()` + `updateThread()`** — Core CRUD operations with zero coverage.
3. **communities role management** — 4 public methods + 1 private helper with zero tests. Entire subsystem untested.
4. **community controller** — 15 endpoints without even basic delegation tests. Any refactor could silently break routing.
5. **community-notes `rateNote()` auto-promote** — The threshold-based status change is the core feature of community notes and has no test.
