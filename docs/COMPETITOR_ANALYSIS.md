# Mizanly — Competitor Gap Analysis (2026-03-09)

## Executive Summary

Mizanly covers ~60% of core social features across its 5 spaces. The remaining 40% represents the gap between "functional app" and "billion-dollar platform."

Research conducted against: TikTok, Instagram, X/Twitter, YouTube, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram.
